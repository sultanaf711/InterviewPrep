Acronyms & Abbreviations (Support, ITSM, Vendor Management)

MTTR
Mean Time To Repair/Resolve
How quickly incidents are resolved; lower MTTR = faster recovery.
Average time to restore service after an incident.
Formula: Total downtime √∑ number of incidents.
Key KPI for support and QBRs.
MI
Major Incident
High-impact incident needing urgent, cross-team response.
Triggers special escalation, war-room, and post-incident review processes.
Often called "P1" or "Sev-1".
Requires structured comms and RCA.
DI MM
Documented Interest Medical Model
Ensures actions/changes are based on documented needs, not assumptions.
Used in regulated/GxP environments for compliance and traceability.
All support actions linked to documented requests.
Critical for audit-readiness.
QBR
Quarterly Business Review
Formal review with vendors on performance, SLAs, KPIs, and CAPAs.
Drives accountability, contract compliance, and continuous improvement.
Includes metrics, open issues, and action plans.
Essential for vendor management.
SLA / OLA
Service/Operational Level Agreement
Defines performance targets (uptime, response time) for services and teams.
SLA: Between provider and customer.
OLA: Between internal teams to support SLAs.
Foundation for support contracts.
CSAT
Customer Satisfaction
Measures user satisfaction with support/service.
Collected via surveys after incidents or service requests.
Used to drive improvements.
Key KPI for support teams.
CAPA
Corrective and Preventive Action
Actions to fix and prevent recurrence of issues.
Tracked after incidents or QBRs.
Ensures issues are resolved and do not repeat.
Required for compliance and improvement.
CMDB / CI
Configuration Management Database / Item
Tracks IT assets and their relationships for support and change control.
CMDB: Repository of all CIs.
CI: Any tracked component (hardware, software, docs).
Critical for incident and change management.
ITSM
IT Service Management
Framework for managing IT services (incident, problem, change, etc.).
Ensures structured, repeatable support and operations.
Includes processes, tools, and governance.
Industry standard for IT support.
PIR
Post-Incident Review
Structured review after an incident to analyze root cause and define fixes.
Ensures learnings are captured and actions are tracked.
Prevents repeat issues.
Key for continuous improvement.
GxP
Good Practice (Pharma/Regulated)
Regulatory standards for systems impacting product quality or patient safety.
Includes GMP, GCP, GLP, etc.
Requires validation, traceability, and compliance.
Critical for pharma/regulated IT.
ELS
Early Life Support
Extra support period after go-live to stabilize new/changed services.
Ensures smooth transition to BAU (Business as Usual).
Monitors incidents and user feedback closely.
Best practice for service transition.
KPI
Key Performance Indicator
Quantitative metric to measure performance (e.g., MTTR, SLA, CSAT).
Used to track, report, and improve service and support outcomes.
Central to dashboards and QBRs.
CAB / BRB
Change Advisory Board / Business Review Board
Governance bodies for approving changes/releases and reviewing business impact.
CAB: Approves technical changes.
BRB: Reviews business impact and readiness.
Ensures safe, controlled change management.
